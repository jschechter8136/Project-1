Jackie Schechter

My name is Jackie Schechter and my interest in photography started in high school when I took a basic photography class. I continued to take photography throughout high school and in my senior year I took AP Photography. My photography focuses on both the natural world and on artistic glass. I started out with an interest in the natural world which included producing photography of plants, animals, and of man made nature scenes around the St. Louis area. In addition, my concentration on artistic glass photography takes a larger piece of glass and focuses in on the small details of the glass to create a new image. Some of the glass photography pieces focus on blown glass or sculptures made from glass and other pieces focus on found glass that was not originally deigned for the purpose of art.

The pieces that are displayed on the General Photography and Glass Concentration pages are all available for purchase. If you don't see a piece that you want to purchase right now, check back later for new pieces that will be available for purchase at a later date.
